The Process

Making your app or website doesn't have to be nerve-racking, I'll be transparent throughout the entire process.

  • Consultation

    Right off the bat, we'll run through ideas, site requirements, time contraints, and budget.

  • Proposal

    Then, we'll propose a contract detailing our agreements, taking into consideration everthing we talked about in our consultation.

  • Development

    After that, I will begin building your site, with weekly checkins.

  • Delivery

    Next, I'll transfer the site and all associated files into your possession.

  • Launch & Support

    Last but certainly not least, we'll make your site live and I'll offer you any ongoing support if needed.
